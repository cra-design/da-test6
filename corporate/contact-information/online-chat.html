CRA Online chat

About online chat in My Account

The Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) offers an online chat service within the My Account portal, allowing taxpayers to discuss account-specific issues with a live CRA agent. To use the chat service, taxpayers need to sign in to their CRA account. The chat widget can be found at the bottom right corner of the page after signing in.

If you are unable to obtain the information you need through the online chat service, you may continue to contact us through our self-serve options or our phone lines.

Hours of service

The online chat service is available in My Account Monday to Friday from 8:00 am to 8:00 pm ET.

Available topics

The online chat service in My Account is available for both account-specific and general questions about:

  • Personal Income Tax
  • Benefits
  • My Account
  • Ordering CRA forms and publications
  • Payments to the CRA
  • Savings and Pension Plans
  • Instalments

Unavailable topics

Agents on the online chat service in My Account are unable to assist with questions about:

  • Rental Income
  • Estates and Trusts
  • Deceased individuals
  • Non-resident returns or enquiries
  • Foreign income reporting
  • Capital Gains
  • Crypto-assets
  • My Business Account
  • Represent a Client
  • Business income or expenses

Questions regarding these topics must continue to be directed towards our phone lines.
